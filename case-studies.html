e-commerce

Price Comparision Analysis

InvanaBot can be deployed on multiple sites like Amazon, Flipkart etc to extract the period prices for an item.

SEO Audit Tool

Deploy crawlers on multiple sites and get insights of the Search Engine Optimisation data and audit how the data is performing.

Amazon Search Results Scraping

Extract the results you'd receive for the set of keywords you give as a query in Amazon.com.

Reviews and Questions Scraping from multiple sources

Extract all the reviews and questions data of a product from various sites like Amazon, Flipkart, ebay etc.

Stock Market Analysis

Marketing

Lead Generation

Deploy crawlers accross social platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin to find who are looking for the service you are providing. Find phone numbers, emails, address information from local and social websites.

Geo Location Data

Scrape all the shops, restaurants, hotels and any information regarding a local place that let's you understand the needs for based on Localised Intelligence.

Competitor Analysis

Follow and understand your competitor's presence on all the social media platforms, that helps you make a right business move.

Real estate

Geo location based Property Aggregation

Deploy crawlers on real estate websites like housing.com, 99acres.com, all the builders and construction ventures websites and aggregate the properties based on the type, value, location etc.

Research

Scrape Search Engines

Aggregate twitter data based on the topics of your research interest for building Machine Learning Models.
