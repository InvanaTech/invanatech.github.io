Our Process

Inspired by Design Thinking Process

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation that draws from the designer's toolkit to integrate the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

Get Started with a Plan

Every problem is unique, and solving them takes different amount of time depending on its complexity. To solve a problem, we need to look into its roots, do research and validate our ideas. Without a proper plan, we can easily get lost in the time. So we need to understand the product vision, business goals, constraints from technology and market along with a powerful process to make a perfect and detailed plan; which ultimately helps in smooth execution. Our process explained below is very fluid in nature and always focuses on bringing best results for the capital spent.

1. Research and Explore

Once we understand the products vision, we start with research to understand the user needs and our opportunities in the market. And we define the personas and scenarios accordingly.

  • User Research
  • Personas and Scenarios
  • Competitor Analysis

2. Information Architecture.

From the research we will now be able to define the problem through mind mapping and we can do few low fidelity iterations and ideate with the help of journey maps. We can now have a proper hypothesis defined for our product.

  • Mind Mapping
  • Journey Mapping
  • Low fidility sketches/wireframes
  • Content draft

3. Prototype

Once we have our hypothesis defined, we can start working on a proof of concept and a clickable prototype to test with our users. After the feedback and iterations, we can freeze our product specifications and document them.

  • High fidility wireframes
  • Clickable prototypes
  • Final Content
  • Style Guide

4. Developing the Technology Solution

Once the specifications are freezed, we build a developer plan of action for our Engineers. We use the tools that allow us to track the progress, see the risks, constantly deploy and maintain the quality of the final product.

  • Constant Code Reviews
  • CI/CD for quick code prototyping
  • Developer & Testing Documentation
  • Building data analytics dashboards
  • ML/DL Models if needed.
