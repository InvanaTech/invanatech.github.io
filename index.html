What we Offer!

Graph Data Management

Build, Visualise graphs and manage knowledge graphs at scale using events from your apps or any data stream using REST APIs.

Transformation Functions

Deploy workers, listen to data streams and transform your streams into knowledge graphs in near real-time.

Event Store

Never worry about Audit or migrate your data to new Schema. Your events are stored for future.

Topology editor

Re-build Knowledge graphs to suite your needs using the event store and transformation functions again and again.
