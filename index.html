Invana is an open-source Graph Data Management System with RESTful Engine for building scalable Knowledge Graphs.

Stay up to date with the updates, from us over medium and twitter

What we Offer!

We offer a Knowledge Management System loaded with data importers, visualisations and prediction engines. Let your applications talk to Invana via RESTful APIs, and as always 'Do your wonders with it'.

Graph Explorer

Run queries and visualise as force directed graphs and tables. Interactively extend your curiosity edge by edge, browsing between each and every connection.

Transformation Functions

Deploy workers, listen to data streams and transform your streams or batch data into knowledge graphs in near real-time.

Topology Management

Update topology and Re-build Knowledge graphs to suite your new needs using the event store and transformation functions again and again.

Event Store

Never worry about Audit or migrate your data to new Schema. Your events are stored for future. So, rebuild events into new graphs anytime.

Marketplace

Feed your curiosity with data pre build knowledge graphs from open source data.

Developer APIs

Build and integrate with work flows that add value to your data by integrating Invana Knowledge Engines with your favorite tools.
